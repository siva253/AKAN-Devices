M2M Connecting

M2M communication is mostly used for remote monitoring. M2M communication plays an important aspect of warehouse management, remote control, traffic control, logistic, robotics services, fleet management, supply chain management and telemedicine. It forms the basis for the Internet of Things (IoT). The key components of an M2M system include RFID, sensors, a Wi-Fi or cellular communications link & autonomic computing software which are programmed n such a manner that it helps all networked device to interpret data and make decisions. The most used type of M2M communication is telemetry, which is in use since the last century to transmit operational data. Presently, M2M do not have any standardized connected device platform & mostly all M2M systems are built to be task- or device-specific. It is also expected that all M2M would become more pervasive and that vendors may need to agree upon standards for device-to-device communications.
